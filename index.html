How it works

1 — Ask

Enter a query like “Istanbul weather and summary”. Orgena routes to relevant open sources.

2 — Fetch

Instant Answers, Wikipedia, Wikidata, and Open‑Meteo respond. Reader view fills any gaps.

3 — Structure

Results are formatted in markdown, tables, and linked references — ready to copy.

4 — Share

Publish or paste the output. Clean, verifiable, and portable.
